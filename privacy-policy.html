Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. General Information

This website respects the privacy of its visitors. This policy describes how data related to navigation and received communications are processed, where applicable, under Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and Organic Law 3/2018, of December 5, on the Protection of Personal Data and guarantee of digital rights (LOPDGDD).

2. Data Collected

This website does not collect personal data automatically. No forms, tracking cookies, or analytics tools that collect identifying data are used.

Only data that the user voluntarily decides to provide when contacting via email will be processed, and solely for the purpose of handling that communication.

3. Cookies

The website uses, where applicable, only technical cookies strictly necessary for its correct functioning. No analytical, advertising, or profiling cookies are used.

4. Purpose and Legal Basis

When the user initiates communication with the site, their data is processed for the purpose of managing that inquiry. The legal basis for processing is the consent of the data subject, manifested by voluntarily sending the communication (Art. 6.1.a GDPR).

5. Retention

Received data is retained only for the time necessary to handle the inquiry and, subsequently, for the statute of limitations period for any potential legal liabilities.

6. Recipients

Data is not shared with third parties, except where legally required. Where applicable, technical service providers (hosting, email) may act as data processors, subject to corresponding contracts. No international data transfers are made to countries outside the European Economic Area.

7. User Rights

The user may exercise, at any time, the rights of access, rectification, erasure, objection, restriction of processing, and portability recognized by data protection regulations, as well as withdraw previously granted consent. Likewise, they have the right to file a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (www.aepd.es) if they believe that the processing of their data violates applicable regulations.

8. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are applied to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information, including encryption of communications via the HTTPS protocol.

9. Modifications

This policy may be updated at any time. Any changes will be published on this page with the date of last update.
