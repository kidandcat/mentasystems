# Police Rentals Platform

Airbnb-style platform for members of security forces in Spain.

## Opportunity

- **Contact:** Individual with low budget
- **Asset:** Manages a Telegram group with **60,000 members**
- **Current usage:** Members share houses/accommodations among police via Telegram
- **Opportunity:** Build a formal platform, monetize with commissions

## Why it's interesting

- 60k users = validated demand + guaranteed distribution
- Trust community (all are verified police officers)
- Niche with no direct competition
- Potential for recurring revenue

## Proposed partnership models

| Model | We receive | Risk |
|-------|------------|------|
| **Equity partner** | 40-50% of the company | High risk, high reward |
| **Revenue share** | 2-3% of each transaction | Lower ownership, passive income |
| **Hybrid** | €5-8k upfront + 20-30% equity | Covers costs + upside |
| **Tech co-founder** | 50/50, we build, they bring users | Real partnership |

## Recommendation

Request **40-50% equity** or **tech co-founder** status. It's not just building an app — it's building a business together. The Telegram group is their contribution, the platform is ours.

## Points to discuss with them

1. Who is the owner? (Create an SL together?)
2. Revenue model: booking commission (3-5%?) or subscription?
3. Who manages support, legal, moderation?
4. Exit strategy if one wants to leave?

## Potential business model

### Revenue streams
- Booking commission: 3-5% to host + 3-5% to guest
- Premium verification / featured listings
- Rental insurance (partnership with insurer)

### Costs
- Hosting: ~€50-100/month initial
- Payment gateway: ~2% Stripe
- Legal/tax: to be determined

### Projection (example)
- 60k members, 5% active = 3,000 active users
- 500 bookings/month x €100 average x 6% commission = €3,000/month
- Growth potential if expanding to other forces (Guardia Civil, firefighters, healthcare workers...)
